FAQ

Quests
  • Q: Can I complete a certain quest for the XP reward in a certain skill?
    • A: If your task only requires you to level up certain skills, you can't start and complete quests to train them.
  • Q: When can I finish a quest?
    • A: When your active task requires you to start a quest, AND you have all the requirements for the quest, you are allowed to complete it.
  • Q: I need a certain amount of Quest Points to complete a requirement, what should I do?
    • Check out Quest List
Clues
  • Q: What is the best way to get each tier of clue scroll?
    • A: Beginner -> Goblins by lumbridge or Minotaurs (Stronghold) if you’ve completed the full sceptre.
    • A: Easy -> Pickpocket Female HAM Members
    • A: Medium -> Kill Guards (Varrock or Falador) if you’re below 50 Hunter (Aerial Fishing task) otherwise catch Eclectic Implings.
    • A: Hard -> Hellhounds in Witchhaven Dungeon (See Family Crest Quest Guide for how to unlock door). If you have all 3 Totem Pieces and an Ancient Shard you can kill them in Catacombs.
  • Q: What clue steps can you drop?
    • A: In Easy Tier you must drop: Fairy Queen, Rune Boots, Regal Pomp (soundtrack). Dropping the Amulet of Glory clue is not required but is highly recommended.
    • A: Any clue scroll which requires a clue reward you do not have i.e. headband / crozier must be dropped.
  • Q: I got an Easy Clue Uniques task but I’m only level 1 thieving?
    • A: You’re allowed to train to 15 thieving to begin this task.
  • Q: Can I build stash units for Uri Clues?
    • A: Provided you ALREADY have the required construction level, you can build STASH units as you receive the clue steps. NOT all at once.
  • Q: Can I complete Regicide when I get a clue in Zul-Andra?
    • A: No, the only reason to complete regicide is for the La Mort Hard Clue Step.
  • Q: Do I need to complete Troll Romance for the Hells Bells song?
    • A: No, you can walk the back way to the bottom of the snow which unlocks the track.
  • Q: I need to unlock the Chaos Altar sound track but don’t have a chaos talisman.
    • A: You are allowed to complete the Enter The Abyss miniquest for this step.
  • Q: I need a gold bar for my clue step how do I get one?
    • A: Get 33 magic and telegrab one from under Varrock West Bank
  • Q: I need a diamond to craft a ring/amulet for a clue, how do I get one?
    • A: Ogresses
  • Q: I need an Addy Square Shield for a clue, what is the best thing to kill for it?
    • A: Named trolls at Death Plateau (no quest req) or Ice Giants in the Rev Caves.
  • Q: Can I get 37 prayer for Sara Wizards?
    • A: no, they can be out distanced by using a shortbow on long range and using a body blocker.
  • Q: I need Lava/Mist runes for Swan song, how do I get them?
    • A: Purchase from Mage Training Arena – total cost is 20 Telekentic, Alch, Grave and 300 enchant points.
Minigames
  • Q: I need to buy a piece of this skilling outfit, which one do I buy?
    • A: Always go cheapest first. Examples of this are Graceful and the Farmer's Set.
  • Q: I need to do castle wars but I only have 1 account.
    • A: Castle wars is F2P, so you can just make another account. You just play against yourself and catch the flag once per game with your task account.
  • Q: Can I sell back Castle Wars armour?
    • A: No, we keep all Castle Wars armour.
  • Q: For the red decorative set which pieces do I have to purchase?
    • A: All pieces including both the platelegs/skirt.
  • Q: Am I allowed to play LMS to get Rune arrows for gp?
    • A: No, the only things you are allowed to purchase from LMS are the Ring of Wealth and Magic Shortbow Scrolls provided you have the items.
  • Q: When doing a pest control task I struggle to maintain %, what can I do?
    • A: Bring an axe to cut the trees and repair barricades for easy %.
  • Q: When on a task which requires farming, can I do Tithe?
    • A: Yes, provided you don’t spend the pts from your runs on any item in the collection log.
  • Q: I got a black satchel task, how do I get a raw swordfish?
    • A: Train to 30 crafting and complete 100% Piscarillius favour to unlock the shop.
  • Q: I need to get two ancient pages how can I do this safetly?
    • A: Ancient page safespot guide:
      1. Enter Ancient Cavern.
      2. Wait 10 minutes to lose aggro. Runelite's "NPC Aggression Timer" plugin can show you how long you need to wait. (You don't have to, but you risk getting slapped by a dragon on the way down)
      3. Step on the marked tile. That tile is inconveniently one step outside the aggro range.
      4. Run back up to the entrance immediately after. You can try to take the aggro of a waterfiend to save a dragon slap.
      5. Wait another 10 minutes.
      6. Go back on that tile.
      7. You can now loot the skeleton from that tile without getting attacked from spawning skeletons. Hop and repeat.
      8. ???
      9. Profit!
      10. PS: The rummage damage scales slightly with your current hitpoints. If you only eat when below 10 hp, you can't die and get more rummages per inventory of food.
  • Q: I need bolt racks, can I kill all barrows brothers?
    • A: Yes, you're allowed to kill all brothers and get 100% for increased odds if you're able to
  • Q: I don’t want to participate in Last Man Standing, can I choose to not do these tasks?
    • A: Yes, you’re allowed to manually complete all LMS tasks while staying an official task only.
Combat/Slayer
  • Q: Can I bury bones on task?
    • A: Yes, as long as you don't go out of your way to use them on an altar or at the ectophial.
  • Q: Can I pick up Numulite/Tokkul/Coins?
    • A: Yes, you can pick this up and store it.
  • Q: Can I fish and cook food?
    • A: You cannot fish for food. You can however cook raw food if you have the level already (in moderation)
  • Q: I just started, what can I use for food?
    • A: Three good possibilities are wines from Draynor (recommended if not using melee), kebabs or gnome food from the Grand Tree.
  • Q: What kind of upgrades are you allowed to buy?
    • A: Anything that is sold. Think about rune sword once you have access to Champion's Guild and green dragonhide in Nardah.
  • Q: I need to train magic, what is the best monster to train on?
    • A: Start with training on zamorak warriors so you can get a rune scimitar for melee training. After that, it doesn't really matter.
  • Q: I need to get an Ancient Shard / Dark Totem Piece, what is the best monster to kill?
    • A: Skeletons unless you have received both the Long Bone and Giant key then you may kill Hill Giants.
  • Q: I need to kill the Mole for its Skin/Claw, how will I do this?
    • A: Train to 30 range (chickens, cows) and purchase some Adamant Arrows + Maple bow from Lowe (Varrock), this kill may take ~2hours, having an alt with Fally Hard+ may help.
  • Q: I need to complete Fremmenik Trials / BA / In Aid of the Myreque but my combat is too low, can I train it?
    • A: Yes you can train your levels to base 30s to assist you in completing this task.
  • Q: I need to start my slayer grind. Can I do the history quiz in the museum for the first 9 levels?
    • A: Yes
  • Q: Can I do wilderness slayer if I must train slayer for my task?
    • A: Yes
  • Q: I have a Dark Mystic Gloves task but have not completed Priest in Peril, what do I do?
    • A: You kill Twisted Banshees in the catacombs.
  • Q: I have a Mystic Hat Light task and need to get the slayer requirement, what do I kill?
    • A: Train slayer till 35 and then kill Wall Beasts
  • Q: What do I kill Turoths with?
    • A: If you have a magic shortbow, use broad arrows with that. Otherwise buy a leafbladed spear from a slayer master.
  • Q: I have the Obor task, what is the best location to farm keys?
    • A: In the Wilderness, in a dungeon a bit west of Mage Bank. The droprate for keys in wilderness is twice as good as outside of it.
  • Q: I need to get a specific slayer task (for example, granite dust task), can I do 9 Turael tasks and then the 10th task at Konar for points?
    • A: No. If you need a specific slayer task, use your points for skips to get it, and otherwise use Turael skips until you get it. Turael skipping means you get a task, and if it isn't the task you need, you cancel it by doing a Turael task. Make sure to block Kalphites before doing so!
  • Q: Can I kill boss variants on a regular slayer task?
    • A: When receiving a slayer task with a boss variant (such as gargoyles / kalphites) you are allowed to do a max. of 35 of the boss variant. (This does not apply when receiving a unique from that certain boss is your active task, or when the task is Kraken.)

    • A: Yes, much like other utility items, you can do this in moderation.

Low agility/magic farming guide for Task Accounts
Overview

This guide is for task accounts that need early farming levels, especially levels 1-34, and have few available teleports. Once you hit 34 Farming, if you still need more levels, you’re allowed (and advised) to get 100% Hosidius favor and do the rest of your training at Tithe Farm.

General Strategy

The bulk of your experience will come from making supercompost by buying pineapples from charter ships and using the supercompost on your plants. The other xp will come from planting and harvesting crops.

*Note: This guide does not include farming hops during your farm runs. While doing early-level farming myself, I found I didn’t have enough time for them, so I’m skipping their inclusion.

Initial Preparation
  • Before starting your farm runs, you’ll want to do some preparation.
    • Buy a boatload of pineapples from charter ships or the Grand Tree food store. Catherby or Port Khazard charter stores are both good options because they’re close to a bank. 200-300 is a good starting amount.
    • Get some allotment and flower seeds (instructions below). A hundred or so are good to start with.
    • Buy 4 or 5 bucket packs from Sarah in the Falador farming store (in the house west of the farming patches) and deposit the buckets into a tool leprechaun at any farming spot. These are for holding supercompost.
    • Get some cash. Charters are not cheap, but they’re the best way to get around with no teles. Getting an inventory of steel plates in the wilderness with a looting bag is the most straightforward way to do this.
Obtaining Seeds
  1. Do you have 38 thieving?
    • Pickpocket Master Farmers
  2. Otherwise, buy seeds from Olivia in Draynor Village. The best type of allotment seed she stocks is cabbage, and the best flower seed she stocks is rosemary.
What to do at the farms

Time to actually gain xp. Do this by running to any number of allotment patches and doing the following actions:

  • Withdraw buckets from the leprechaun, grab the 15 supercompost you made last run, then deposit the super compost into the leprechaun.
  • Use 15 more pineapples on the compost bin to start making more super compost.
  • Harvest your completed crops, and use them on the leprechaun to turn them into notes and save inventory space.
  • Plant your allotment and flower seeds. Use your supercompost on them. Not only does this keep the plants alive, but you get xp for using the compost.
  • Head to the next patch!
Which patches should I use?

There are 4 patches you can consider using for early game training:

  • Falador farm
  • Catherby
  • Ardy farm
  • Hosidius farm

You don’t have to do all of them. Maybe you only have the time/run energy to do three of the patches. That’s fine. Or you might find it too annoying to run all the way to Hosidius. That’s cool too.

With zero teleports, one possible rotation can look like:
  1. Home teleport to Lumbridge
  2. Run to the Falador Farm.
  3. Farm in Falador.
  4. Run to Port Sarim, and take the charter to Catherby.
  5. Farm in Catherby.
  6. Take the charter back to Port Sarim
  7. Run west to the boat in Rimmington that takes you to the Ardougne/Brimhaven. Take it to Ardougne. Run to the Ardougne farming patch.
  8. Farm in Ardougne.
  9. Minigame tele to Pest Control, then take the boat back to Port Sarim
  10. Run to Veos in the northernmost boat section, and take the boat to Zeah.
  11. Run to the Hosidius farming patch.
  12. Farm in Hosidius.
  13. Repeat!

Once you get some teleports, this can be shortened dramatically. Falador and Camelot teleports really help out, as does the Xeric’s Talisman teleport. Also, once you go into Tithe Farm one time, you can start using the Tithe Farm minigame tele for future farm runs.

Tip: If you get a Garden Pie from Young or Gourmet implings or the Cooking Guild, you can boost to get into Tithe Farm at 31 Farming to unlock the teleport 3 levels early.
